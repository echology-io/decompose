01

Most companies automate

Vendors sell products. Your company needs strategy. Without knowing which workflows benefit from AI — in what order, at what risk — you get wasted budget, skeptical teams, and no measurable improvement.

Our discovery process answers one question:
Where does automation actually help your business?

02

Every workflow gets scored
on four questions.

We ask each question about every workflow your team identifies. Score 1–5 on each. Add them up. Highest totals go first.

01 — Clarity

Is this workflow well-defined?

Clear start and end points? Consistent inputs and outputs? Documented rules? The more structured the workflow, the higher it scores.

1 = Ad-hoc, subjective5 = Standardized, repeatable
02 — Reach

How many people does it touch?

A workflow that affects every department and every project scores higher than one used occasionally by a single team.

1 = One person, rarely5 = Company-wide, daily
03 — Value

What happens if we improve it?

Time saved. Errors caught. Revenue protected. Deadlines held. We're looking for material outcomes, not marginal convenience.

1 = Nice to have5 = Protects revenue or compliance
04 — Feasibility

How realistic is automation?

Good data available? Rules are clear? Edge cases are manageable? Not every workflow is ready — we score this honestly.

1 = High complexity, high risk5 = Straightforward

04

Five steps.
Two weeks.

This is a working session, not a presentation. We build the roadmap together.

1

You talk, we listen.

You describe what slows your company down — manual data entry, compliance checks, report generation, knowledge silos. We capture every candidate workflow.

2

We score together.

Each workflow gets scored on all four questions. You provide the domain context. We provide automation feasibility. Every score is transparent.

3

Priorities emerge.

Scores sort. Workflows cluster into phases. You see which ones share infrastructure, which are prerequisites, and where the quick wins sit.

4

We validate with your real work.

We review live documents and processes from your active operations to ground-truth every workflow score against how your team actually works today.

5

You get the roadmap.

A structured document with every workflow scored, ranked, and phased. Your leadership can evaluate it without us in the room.
