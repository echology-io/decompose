The Problem

Most firms start in the wrong place.

AEC firms are told they need AI. Vendors sell them products. What they actually need is a clear understanding of which workflows benefit from automation, in what order, and at what risk.

Without that, firms either automate the wrong things, underestimate complexity, or stall entirely. The result is the same: wasted budget, skeptical teams, and no measurable improvement.

Phase 1 Discovery solves this. Before any technology decision, we score every candidate workflow against four dimensions and produce a ranked roadmap your leadership can defend.

The Engagement

How Phase 1 works.

A collaborative working session, not a presentation. We score your workflows together and walk out with a prioritized roadmap.

1

Listen

You describe the workflows that slow your firm down. Document review bottlenecks, spec compliance gaps, knowledge silos, manual QC processes. We capture every candidate as a scored row.

"What slows you down the most?"
2

Score

Each workflow is scored collaboratively across all four W.A.V.E. dimensions. You provide domain context. We provide automation feasibility assessment. The scoring is transparent — you see every number and why.

"Would you say this affects everyone or just power users?"
3

Rank

Composite scores sort automatically. Workflows cluster into phases. Patterns emerge: which workflows share infrastructure, which are prerequisites for others, where the highest-value opportunities sit.

"Based on these scores, here's what I'd start with and why."
4

Deliver

You receive a complete roadmap document with every workflow scored, ranked, and annotated. Implementation phases sequenced by dependency and value. A decision framework your leadership team can evaluate without us in the room.

5

Validate

We process a representative sample of your documents through the AECai pipeline — parsing, classification, standards cross-referencing, entity extraction — and include the results as evidence supporting the roadmap priorities.
